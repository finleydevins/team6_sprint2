Trick or Treating Facts and Popularities!

Trick-or-treating is a Halloween tradition where children dress up in costumes and go door-to-door in their neighborhoods, asking for candy and treats. The phrase "trick or treat" implies that if treats are not given, a trick may be played, though this is rarely taken seriously. The activity typically takes place on the evening of October 31 and is a fun way for communities to celebrate Halloween, fostering a sense of togetherness and festive spirit. In addition to candy, some households may offer small toys or other goodies.
